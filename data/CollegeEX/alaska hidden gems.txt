Alaska Pacific University (Anchorage, AK): A small university with around 200 full-time undergraduates, APU is situated on the edge of wilderness in Anchorage, which provides for an intimate educational setting, minutes from the expansive outdoors of Alaska's mountains, seas, and wilderness.
University of Alaska, Southeast (Juneau, AK): UAS delivers a small-college, residential experience akin to a liberal arts college for the price of a public university. Located right on the ocean, this compact campus in the northernmost temperate rain forest abounds in educational and recreational opportunities despite its size.
