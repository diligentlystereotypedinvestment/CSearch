Bellarmine University (Louisville, KY): A small, comprehensive, Catholic university in Louisville, Bellarmine offers excellent preparation, involved faculty, and an attractive campus with the burgeoning city of Louisville at its doorstep.
Berea College (Berea, KY): Berea provides a rigorous liberal arts education with a creative emphasis as well as training in crafts, and all this at an unbeatable price. A "work college," Berea is alone among top US colleges to aware every student enrolled a no-tuition promise.
Transylvania University (Lexington, KY): Transylvania is a rigorous yet supportive small liberal arts college in the heart of Lexington.
