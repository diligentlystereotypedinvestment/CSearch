Central Washington University (Ellensburg, WA): Central Washington University is home to an excellent program in construction management.
University of Puget Sound (Tacoma, WA): Puget Sound has a successful history of writing curriculum across disciplines as well as a well-regarded Honors business program and School of Physical Therapy. The university's campus is beautiful, with themed dorms and easy access to Seattle.
University of Washington, Bothell (Bothell, WA): Students at UW Bothell reap the benefits of UW Seattle's excellent curriculum without the flagship's large class sizes. UW Bothell's excellent business program offers both BA and MBA degrees.
Washington State University (Pullman, WA): Washington State is home to a fantastic Honors program plus a seven-year program in veterinary medicine. A setting just seven miles west of the University of Idaho in Moscow makes for a lively community and plenty of student-oriented businesses, restaurants, and venues.
Whitman College (Walla Walla, WA): Whitman is a rigorous liberal arts college with exceptional geology and English programs. Its unique curriculum includes a first-year sequence that every Whitman student completes.
