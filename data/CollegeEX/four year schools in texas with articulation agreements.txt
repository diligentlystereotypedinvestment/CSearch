Abilene Christian University (Abilene, TX): At least one-quarter of the total semester hours needed to graduate must be completed at the university.
Baylor University (Waco, TX): Credit by examination is reevaluated based on Baylor's policies. It is not taken from the college transcript; student must submit official score reports (AP, CLEP, etc.).
Hardin&#8211;Simmons University (Abilene, TX): Transfer students must submit their final high school transcripts and official ACT or SAT scores if they have fewer than 24 transferable hours.
Lamar University (Beaumont, TX): Students with fewer than 18 semester hours must meet freshmen admission requirements.
LeTourneau University (Longview, TX): Three letters of recommendation required.
Our Lady of the Lake University (San Antonio, TX): Applicants with fewer than 30 semester hours enter as freshmen.
Rice University (Houston, TX): Recommendations from two professors and a statement of good standing from Dean of Students required. Portfolio required of architecture program applicants; audition required of music program applicants.
Sam Houston State University (Huntsville, TX): TASP required.
University of Houston &#8212; Clear Lake (Houston, TX): College algebra, Texas Academic Skills Program (TASP) exam required.
University of Texas Medical Branch, Galveston, The (Galveston, TX): Transfer policies vary by school and program.
University of Texas at Dallas (Richardson, TX): Transfer applicants who are classified as freshmen or sophomores will be reviewed using the same criteria as first-time freshmen.
University of the Incarnate Word (San Antonio, TX): Joint admission agreement with Alamo Colleges.
