Marshall University (Huntington, WV): This public, interactive university of approximately 14,000 (including 4,000 graduate and medical students) features two-year and four-year undergraduate and graduate programs. In addition, Marshall has cultivated great school spirit and an excellent town relationship.
West Virginia University (Morgantown, WV): A major research university, this public school of approximately 20,000 students ranks high in Division I sports and great school spirit.
West Virginia Wesleyan College (Buckhannon, WV): Private and affiliated with the Methodist Church, West Virginia Wesleyan offers undergraduate and graduate programs to approximately 1,200 students.
