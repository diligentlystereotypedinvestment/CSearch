Grand Valley State University (Allendale, MI): An easy one to miss, Grand Valley is worth a look for students who think the only quality public universities in the state are the University of Michigan and Michigan State.
Oakland University (Rochester, MI): A wide-ranging curriculum includes schools of nursing and health sciences plus a product life cycle management program through the school of business administration. This place is filled with caring people and lots of help to make the freshmen year a success.
Wayne State University (Detroit, MI): Located in Detroit, Wayne State offers a "real-world" experience and a high-quality education at a reasonable cost.
