Southern Utah University (Cedar City, UT): A transfer student who transfers within the USHE system with an Associate degree, or who has met the General Education requirements of another USHE institution will have their GE requirements met at the receiving institution by USHE policy.
The University of Utah (Salt Lake City, UT): If applicant has less than 30 semester hours of transferable work, secondary school transcript and ACT/SAT scores are required to apply.
