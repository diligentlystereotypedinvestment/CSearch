Drury University (Springfield, MO): This medium-sized university is home to an innovative undergraduate architecture department.
Missouri University of Science and Technology (Rolla, MO): Missouri Tech provides exceptional job placement and strong summer programs in engineering and technology at an affordable price.
Webster University (St. Louis, MO): A midsized university in suburban St. Louis, Webster boasts excellent theater and music departments.
Westminster College (Fulton, MO): Westminster is particularly strong in its extensive programs for students with learning disabilities.
William Jewell College (Liberty, MO): William Jewell offers incredible special opportunities such as the Journey Grant and Oxbridge Honors program. Secluded in the town of Liberty, the college is only 20 minutes from the attractions of Kansas City.
