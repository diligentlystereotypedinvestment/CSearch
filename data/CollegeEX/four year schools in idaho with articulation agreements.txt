Idaho State University (Pocatello, ID): General education requirements waived for transfers holding associate degrees; out-of-state associate degrees considered on an individual basis.
Northwest Nazarene University (Nampa, ID): Students holding A.A. degrees may enter with junior standing and waiver of most general education requirements.
University of Idaho (Moscow, ID): College of Engineering requires a minimum 2.8 GPA for out-of-state transfers.
