St. John's College (Santa Fe, NM): Nestled in the mountains surrounding Santa Fe, St. John's College is a perfect fit for the student who is passionate about knowledge. Taught by "tutors" rather than professors, St. John's discussion-based classes in the liberal arts are grounded in the Great Books curriculum. This institution, along with its sister campus in Annapolis, Maryland, is ideal for those who desire not just a degree but also an education.
